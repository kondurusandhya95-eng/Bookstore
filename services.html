Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sit and read without purchasing?

Members may browse and read freely in our lounge. Non-members are welcome to browse — a small café order keeps you comfortable for as long as you like.

How does book lending work?

Bibliophile Club members can borrow up to 3 books at a time for 3 weeks. Literary Members get 1 borrow per month. Returns are simple — drop off in-store or at our dropbox.

How do I place a pre-order?

Browse upcoming releases in our store or dashboard. Pre-orders can be placed online or in-store, and Bibliophile members get priority over general stock.
